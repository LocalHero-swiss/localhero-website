WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of diverse backgrounds and nationalities, brought together in the Versuvirus Hackathon organized by the Swiss Confederation and ImpactHub.

We are creating a new universe for associations, volunteers and people in need.

Our objective is to connect people in need and volunteers in a simple, smart way.

Connecting associations, institutions and volunteers could make a big difference in times of crisis, by facilitating the deployment of volunteers where they are needed.

In the next few days we will make available our app, that well be useful during this crisis to all those people at risk that cannot leave their homes, and for all the beautiful people out there that are looking forward to help those in need.

We foresee a future in which we will be willing to help those in need when we have some free time. That's our vision and major learning from this crisis, where we concretely saw people in action to help the community.

We believe that thanks to our solution existing volunteering platforms and associations could go hand by hand, collaborating and sharing resources when needed.

With our Central Hero Repository we want to bring unity and, in time of crisis, we want to be a tool to facilitate volunteers deployment.

We are dedicating our free time to bring this dream to life.

Because even with small actions, each of us can become Local Hero!
